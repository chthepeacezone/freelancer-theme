Meet Cheryl - A Kindness Advocate & Change Facilitator

Why choose Cheryl for your Journey?

With over 25 years of experience Social Work and Nursing, Cheryl has worked in community health settings, public and private hospital settings, government, non-governmental organisations and private practice. She specialises in external supervision and career guidance, substance & harm reduction and behaviour change models for individuals, groups and organisations.

Cheryl comes from a blend of different modalities including but not limited to: Motivational Interviewing, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy, and CBT & emotion coaching. Most importantly, Cheryl considers herself a kindness advocate in the human journey.

Cheryl provides BCP courses for VicRoads as an Accredited Behaviour Change Facilitator and she is a registered Social Worker with the AASW.

For inquiries - contact Cheryl.
